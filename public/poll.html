Poll creation form
View polls creation tips.

This form allows you to create new polls. You can enter an opinion you want to poll or title of an election you would like to host.

  • Setting poll to Public allows any registered voter to participate.
  • Setting the poll to Private provides you a 6 character unique poll identifier code. You can share this unique identifier with your closed goup of participants who will be given access to voting via this identifier code.
  • Enableling anonymous voting allows participants to vote without them being identifyable. Their voting data is hashed.
